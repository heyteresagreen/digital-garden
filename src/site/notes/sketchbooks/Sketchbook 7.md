📖 Stillman & Birn Alpha A5
🎨 Daniel Smith watercolours 
✏️ Prismacolor Premier pencils, Derwent Rebel watercolour pencils
✒️ Lamy Safari, Kaweco Sport, Sailor Fude de Manen 
...plus other random materials

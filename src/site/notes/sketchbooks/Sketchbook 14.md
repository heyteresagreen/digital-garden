I started this sketchbook in October 2025 and completed it in March 2026. I unintentionally skipped doing a sketchbook number 13!

📖 Homemade using Artec Como Sketch Pad 210gsm mixed media paper (pocket sized) 
🎨 Daniel Smith watercolours, Winsor & Newton gouache 
✒️ Sailor Fude with permanent black ink, Kaweco Sport with permanent sepia ink
🖌️ 1/4 inch dagger brush
